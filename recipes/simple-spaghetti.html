Lasagna Recipe


Description

A comforting Italian dish made with rich tomato sauce, garlic, and herbs. Simple, flavorful, and ready in minutes.


Ingridients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Drain spaghetti and add it to the sauce.
  6. Toss well and cook for another minute.
  7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan if desired.
