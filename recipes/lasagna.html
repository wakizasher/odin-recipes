Lasagna Recipe


Description

Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese come together in this timeless Italian comfort dish.


Ingridients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 500 g ground beef (or half beef, half pork)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 250 g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Rest and serve
