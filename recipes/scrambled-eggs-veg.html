Lasagna Recipe


Description

Soft, creamy eggs mixed with colorful veggies for a healthy, protein-packed breakfast or light lunch.


Ingridients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add onion, tomato, and bell pepper — cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Stir gently until the eggs are softly cooked.
  6. Serve warm with toast.
