Lasagna Recipe


Description

Tender, juicy chicken baked with crispy golden potatoes and aromatic spices — an easy all-in-one dinner.


Ingridients

  • 2 chicken thighs (or breasts)
  • 3 potatoes, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place chicken and potatoes on a baking tray.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss everything to coat evenly.
  5. Bake for 35–40 minutes, turning once halfway through.
  6. Check if the chicken is cooked through and potatoes are golden.
  7. Garnish with herbs and serve hot.
